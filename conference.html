37th Annual IFIP WG 11.3

Conference on Data and Applications Security and Privacy (DBSec'23)

SAP Labs France, Sophia Antipolis, France - July 19 - 21, 2023

Conference Venue


SAP Security Research, 805 av. Du docteur Maurice Donat, Font de l'Orme, 06250 Mougins

Coming to SLF from the airport: check the details on google maps

From Nice by car: Highway A8 - Exit N°44 towards Cannes/Antibes:

  • Directly after the tollgate take the left exit (“Antibes/Juan-les-Pins”) and follow the signs to Grasse/Valbonne (D35/D103)
  • Stay on the "Route du Parc" (D103), go straight ahead
  • At the roundabout (“Carrefour des Bouillides”) please follow the signs to Font de l’Orme, Mougins (D98)
  • You will find SAP Labs France on the right (approx. 3,5 km after the roundabout "Carrefour des Bouillides")

Rental Hire companies: google maps (rental car highly recommended)
